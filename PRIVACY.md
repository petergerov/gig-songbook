# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2025**

Gig Songbook does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data you enter (songs, setlists, settings) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party tracking.

The only network request the app makes is to load publicly available chord diagram images from a third-party GitHub repository. No personal information is sent in this request.
