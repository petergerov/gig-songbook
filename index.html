Everything you need

Every feature earns its place

No bloat. No subscriptions. Just tools that make your performance smoother.

🌑

Performance Mode

Full-screen dark display built for the stage. Top and bottom bars appear in sync. Tap corners to navigate, left zone for the song picker, anywhere else to toggle bars.

🎙️

Rehearse Mode

Practice in a full-screen view with a searchable sidebar. Select multiple artist and label filters at once — OR-combined, each dismissible as a chip. Filter state carries over from the Songs view automatically.

🎵

Audio Tracks

Attach a reference recording or backing track to any song. Import from Files, iCloud Drive, or directly from your iPhone Music Library (non-DRM tracks). A seekable progress bar shows elapsed and remaining time — tap or drag to jump to any position.

🔍

Smart Multi-Filter

Select multiple artists and labels at once — filters are OR-combined. Active filters appear as chips below the search field; dismiss one at a time or reset all. Filter state syncs between Songs and Rehearse.

🔒

Setlist Locking

Lock a setlist before you walk on stage. Nothing gets accidentally edited, deleted, or reordered.

🎼

Chord Diagrams

Interactive fretboard diagrams for every chord — rendered on-device, no internet needed. Open position, A-shape and E-shape voicings.

✏️

Edit During Performance

Spot a wrong chord mid-show? Edit lyrics and notes directly from Performance mode or Rehearse mode — no need to leave the view. Only available when the setlist is unlocked.

📝

Import from Notes

Share any Apple Notes note directly to Gig Songbook via the iOS Share Sheet. It arrives parsed and ready in your library.

🎶

Demo Setlist

New to the app? Load a ready-made demo setlist in one tap to explore every feature right away. Missed the first-launch prompt? Find it in Settings any time your library is empty.

✈️

Fully Offline

No account. No login. No cloud. Everything lives on your device. Works in a basement, a field, or mid-flight.

🎸

Chord Transposition Pro

Transpose any song up or down in real time. Set fixed transpositions per song in a setlist. Chords update — lyrics never change.

🌐

Online Song Catalog Pro

Search by title or artist, pick multiple results — each showing album and duration to tell versions apart — and import them all at once, lyrics included.

🎧

Import Audio Files Pro

Select one or more audio files (MP3, M4A, WAV, FLAC and more) and let the app read the embedded metadata — title, artist and lyrics are filled in automatically. Review every track before importing, then confirm with a single tap.

📄

Import TXT / PDF / MD Pro

Bring existing lyrics and chord sheets into the app. Select multiple files at once — titles, artists, keys and BPM are parsed from the file content automatically.

📤

Export & Backup Pro

Export your full song library or individual setlists as JSON. Setlist exports embed all songs — AirDrop a complete, self-contained file to a bandmate in seconds.
