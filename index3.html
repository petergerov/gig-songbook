Up and running in 3 steps

No account. No setup. Open the app and start playing.

01 🎵

Add your songs

Type lyrics and chords directly, or import from .md, .txt, and .pdf files. Fetch lyrics automatically by artist and title.

02 📋

Build a setlist

Create a setlist, drag to reorder, add date and venue. Select multiple songs at once and add them all in one tap. Lock it before showtime so nothing moves by accident.

03 🎤

Perform with confidence

Tap Start Performance for a full-screen dark view. Transpose chords live. Navigate songs with corner taps or browse your full library from the sidebar.

Everything you need

Every feature earns its place

Every feature exists for one reason — to make your performance smoother.

🎸

Chord Transposition

Transpose any song up or down in real time during performance. Set fixed transpositions per song in a setlist. Chords update — lyrics never change.

🌑

Setlist Performance

Full-screen dark display built for the stage. Tap the bottom corners to move between songs. A status bar shows your position in the set. Song picker always one swipe away.

🔍

Song Browser Sidebar

Performing a single song? Open the sidebar to browse your full library, filter by artist or label, and switch songs instantly — without leaving performance mode. On iPad, Artist and Label filters appear as collapsible columns.

🔒

Setlist Locking

Lock a setlist before you walk on stage. Nothing gets accidentally edited, deleted, or reordered. Saved transpositions apply automatically.

🎼

Chord Diagrams

Interactive fretboard diagrams for every chord in your song — rendered entirely on-device, no internet needed. Open position, A-shape and E-shape voicings.

📤

Import & Export PRO

Import from files or folders. Export your entire library or individual setlists as self-contained JSON files. AirDrop to bandmates in seconds.

🌐

Online Song Catalog PRO

Search by title or artist, pick multiple results — each showing album and duration to tell versions apart — and import them all at once, lyrics included.

✈️

Fully Offline

No account. No login. No cloud. Everything lives on your device. Works in a basement, a field, or mid-flight.
