# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Data Collection

Gig Songbook does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All data you enter — songs, setlists, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard `UserDefaults` storage and never leaves it.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party tracking.

Chord diagrams are rendered entirely on-device — no network request is made for chord data.

The app makes the following outbound network requests, both of which are user-initiated:

| Request | When | Data sent | Service |
|---|---|---|---|
| Lyrics lookup | Tapping "Fetch Lyrics" in the song editor | Song title and artist name | `api.lyrics.ovh` |
| Lyrics lookup (fallback) | Tapping "Fetch Lyrics" in the song editor | Song title and artist name | `lrclib.net` |

No personal information (name, email, location, device ID, etc.) is ever included in any of these requests. The song title and artist name you provide are used solely to retrieve lyrics and are not stored by this app.

## In-App Purchases

Gig Songbook offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Gig Songbook Pro") processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. The app does not handle or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transactions: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)

## Children

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
