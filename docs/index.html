Simple by design

Up and running in 3 steps

No account. No setup. Open the app and start playing.

01 🎵

Add your songs

Type lyrics and chords directly, or import from .md, .txt, and .pdf files. Fetch lyrics automatically by artist and title.

02 📋

Build a setlist

Create a setlist for your gig, drag to reorder, add date and venue. Lock it before showtime so nothing moves by accident.

03 🎤

Perform with confidence

Tap Start Performance for a full-screen dark view. Transpose chords live with one tap. Swipe between songs.

Everything you need

Built for the stage

Every feature exists for one reason — to make your performance smoother.

🎸

Chord Transposition

Transpose any song up or down in real time during performance. Set fixed transpositions per song in a setlist. Chords update — lyrics never change.

🌑

Performance Mode

Full-screen dark display with auto-hiding controls. Tap to reveal, swipe to navigate. Screen stays on — your phone won't sleep mid-song.

🔒

Setlist Locking

Lock a setlist before you walk on stage. Nothing gets accidentally edited, deleted, or reordered. Saved transpositions apply automatically.

🎼

Chord Diagrams PRO

Interactive fretboard diagrams for every chord in your song — rendered entirely on-device, no internet needed. Open position, A-shape and E-shape voicings.

📤

Import & Export PRO

Import from files or folders. Export your entire library or individual setlists as self-contained JSON files. AirDrop to bandmates in seconds.

✈️

Fully Offline

No account. No login. No cloud. Everything lives on your device. Works in a basement, a field, or mid-flight.
