Song Library

Build your complete repertoire.

Every song you know, in one place. Store lyrics and chords side by side, log the key and BPM, jot performance notes — and tag songs with labels like Rock, Jazz or Blues for instant filtering.

  • Unlimited songs — no cap, no paywall on the library
  • Full-text search by title, artist or label
  • Fetch lyrics automatically from the web with one tap
  • Import from .md, .txt and .pdf files (Pro)

Setlists

Every gig, perfectly ordered.

Create a setlist for each show. Add a date and venue, drag to reorder, set a transposition per song — then lock it before you hit the stage so nothing changes by accident.

  • Unlimited setlists, free forever
  • Per-song transpose saved with each setlist
  • Lock to prevent accidental edits during performance
  • Export individual setlists to share with bandmates

How It Works

Ready in three steps.

01
Build your library

Add songs manually or import whole folders of .md, .txt and .pdf files in seconds. Fetch lyrics with one tap. Tag songs with labels for instant filtering.

02
Create your setlist

Multi-select songs to add them all at once. Drag to reorder. Set a per-song transposition for nights when the band plays in a different key. Lock when ready.

03
Take the stage

Tap bottom corners to navigate songs. Transpose live with a tap. Swipe open the song browser sidebar. Everything stays in sync across your iPhone and iPad via iCloud.

🔒

Your data is yours.

No account. No sign-in. No ads. No analytics. Your songs live on your device and in your private iCloud container — never on any third-party server. Lyrics are fetched on demand only when you explicitly ask; nothing is sent automatically.
